### Product Overview — Rovierr

**Purpose**
Rovierr is a unified platform designed for **university students**, providing a connected ecosystem of academic and lifestyle tools. It empowers students to manage their studies, clubs, and personal lives through modular apps that interconnect seamlessly.

**Target Users**

* University students (undergraduate & postgraduate)
* Student leaders and club organizers
* Educators adopting tech-enabled teaching tools

**Core Value**
Unlike traditional LMS platforms, Rovierr focuses on **students first**, offering intuitive, real-time, and collaborative experiences built for their daily use — inside and outside the classroom.

**Key Features (Initial Modules)**

* Real-time classroom tools (interactive slides, live quizzes, polls)
* University club management tools (events, communication, finances)
* Money management & student budgeting
* Social learning spaces & student communities

**Business Objectives**

* Achieve strong adoption in Hong Kong and Bangladesh university ecosystems
* Build brand trust through student ambassadors and institutional partnerships
* Monetize via premium features, contextual student ads, and SaaS club tools
